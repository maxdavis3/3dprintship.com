Print & Ship Process

01

Standard Print & Ship

Upload STL or STEP, receive an instant quote, and confirm order. Production begins same business day. Parts shipped via standard ground or 2-day express. Tracking provided from dispatch.

02

Rush 24-Hour Service

Need parts fast? Rush production start within 2 hours of order. Overnight delivery available to all US metro areas. Premium rush service at 30% surcharge — still the fastest way to physical parts.

03

Drop-Ship to Your Customer

White-label fulfillment for 3D printing resellers and on-demand businesses. Print parts and ship directly to your customer in plain packaging. No branding on box or packing slip unless requested.
